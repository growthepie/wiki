# Introduction

### 🥧 Welcome to growthepie

growthepie is your go-to source for metrics, analysis, and insights across the Ethereum ecosystem - from Mainnet to Layer 2s and Ethereum-aligned sidechains.

We measure how these networks are used, what drives their growth, and how they contribute to Ethereum’s overall success.

#### Our Mission

We want Ethereum to thrive - and that means helping its scaling solutions and applications succeed.

By providing transparent data on _how_ chains are used and _what for_, we empower Layer 2 teams, app builders, and the broader community to make better decisions, grow sustainably, and align around a shared goal: growing the pie for everyone.

### What We Cover

**The Full Ethereum Stack:** We track everything from Mainnet fundamentals to layer 2s, to data availability layers, and other aligned chains like Polygon PoS (soon).

**Application Metrics:** See what users actually do on each network - from DeFi and gaming to NFTs and social apps. Our goal is to understand _real usage_, not just raw transaction counts.

**Blockspace Analytics:** We go beyond high-level KPIs to analyze how blockspace is used. Which apps dominate activity? Which use cases are growing fastest? How efficiently do chains scale?

**Economics:** How much are chains making in fees, and how much do they have to pay towards DA and settlement,

**Project Comparisons:** Compare chains and ecosystems side-by-side. Understand their trade-offs in cost, decentralization, and adoption.

**Ecosystem Insights:** Get an overview of how Ethereum’s scaling layers evolve - and how together, they strengthen the network’s security, decentralization, and user experience.

### Why growthepie?

The Ethereum landscape moves fast. New Layer 2s launch, applications surge, and activity patterns shift daily.

growthepie helps you cut through the noise with transparent, data-driven insights on how Ethereum scales - in practice.

Whether you’re:

* Building a Layer 2 or app
* Researching network adoption
* Investing in the ecosystem
* Or simply exploring

→ growthepie gives you the clarity and context to navigate Ethereum’s growth story.
